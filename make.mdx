## Getting Started

We have created a Make.com module to help you integrate Pingmee with your automations. This module provides a seamless way to connect Pingmee's messaging and automation features directly within your Make.com scenarios.

<Note>
  To get started, you'll need to install the Pingmee module in Make.com. Click the link below to access the module invitation.
</Note>

## View the Module

[Pingmee Module](https://www.make.com/en/integrations/pingmee)

Once installed, you can add the Pingmee module to your Make.com scenarios and start automating your messaging workflows.

---

## What You Can Do

With the Pingmee Make.com module, you can:

- **Send Messages** - Automatically send messages to customers
- **Manage Conversations** - Handle and respond to customer conversations
- **Create Customers** - Add new customers to your Pingmee account
- **Work with Templates** - Use message templates in your automations
- **Manage Tags** - Organize and categorize your contacts

---

## Authentication

The Pingmee module handles authentication automatically. When you first add the module to a scenario, you'll be prompted to:

1. Connect your Pingmee account
2. Authorize the connection
3. Start building your automation

<Note>
  Make sure you have an active Pingmee account before connecting the module.
</Note>

---

## Next Steps

After installing the module:

1. Create a new scenario in Make.com
2. Add the Pingmee module as a trigger or action
3. Configure your automation workflow
4. Test and activate your scenario
